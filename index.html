About

Human flourishing is a state of overall well-being that's more than just happiness. It's a process of developing and using one's strengths, virtues, and capabilities in different areas of life. It's also a moral basis for what it means to be human.

Plato

Plato literally identifies your flourishing with your excellence, with your moral and intellectual excellence – both flourishing, eudaimonia, and excellence, aretē, are constituted by the same psychic harmony between the three faculties in your psyche.
